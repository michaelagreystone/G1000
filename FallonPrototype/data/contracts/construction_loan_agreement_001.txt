CONSTRUCTION LOAN AGREEMENT

This Construction Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of January 15, 2024, by and between:

LENDER: First National Bank of Massachusetts, a Massachusetts banking corporation, with principal offices at 100 Federal Street, Boston, MA 02110 ("Lender")

BORROWER: Fallon Development LLC, a Delaware limited liability company, with principal offices at 1 Seaport Lane, Suite 500, Boston, MA 02210 ("Borrower")

GUARANTOR: The Fallon Company, LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Guarantor")

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Borrower desires to construct a 350-unit multifamily residential development with ground-floor retail space located at 450 Northern Avenue, Boston, Massachusetts 02210 (the "Project"); and

WHEREAS, Lender is willing to make a construction loan to Borrower upon the terms and conditions set forth herein;

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants herein, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1 - LOAN AMOUNT AND TERMS

1.1 Principal Amount. Lender agrees to lend to Borrower the principal sum of Seventy-Five Million Dollars ($75,000,000.00) (the "Loan").

1.2 Interest Rate. The Loan shall bear interest at a rate equal to the Secured Overnight Financing Rate (SOFR) plus two hundred seventy-five (275) basis points per annum, adjusted monthly.

1.3 Loan Term. The initial term of the Loan shall be thirty-six (36) months from the date of this Agreement, with two (2) six-month extension options subject to satisfaction of extension conditions.

1.4 Loan-to-Cost Ratio. The Loan shall not exceed sixty-five percent (65%) of the total Project costs as approved by Lender.

ARTICLE 2 - DISBURSEMENTS

2.1 Draw Requests. Borrower may submit monthly draw requests for construction costs incurred, accompanied by:
    (a) AIA Form G702/G703 certified by the general contractor
    (b) Lien waivers from all contractors and subcontractors
    (c) Inspection report from Lender's construction consultant
    (d) Updated title insurance endorsement

2.2 Retainage. Lender shall retain ten percent (10%) of each disbursement until the Project achieves Substantial Completion.

ARTICLE 3 - INTEREST RESERVE

3.1 Interest Reserve Account. An interest reserve in the amount of Five Million Dollars ($5,000,000.00) shall be established at closing and funded from Loan proceeds.

ARTICLE 4 - EQUITY REQUIREMENTS

4.1 Minimum Equity. Borrower shall contribute a minimum of Thirty-Five Million Dollars ($35,000,000.00) in equity prior to Lender's first disbursement.

4.2 Equity First. Borrower's equity shall be contributed first, before any Loan disbursements.

ARTICLE 5 - COMPLETION GUARANTY

5.1 Guarantor's Obligations. Guarantor unconditionally guarantees completion of the Project in accordance with the approved plans and specifications, free of liens, within the construction budget.

5.2 Cost Overrun Guaranty. Guarantor guarantees payment of all Project costs in excess of the Loan amount and approved budget.

ARTICLE 6 - DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

6.1 Events of Default. The following shall constitute Events of Default:
    (a) Failure to make any payment when due
    (b) Material breach of any representation, warranty, or covenant
    (c) Bankruptcy or insolvency of Borrower or Guarantor
    (d) Abandonment of construction
    (e) Failure to achieve Substantial Completion within the Loan term

6.2 Remedies. Upon an Event of Default, Lender may:
    (a) Accelerate all amounts due
    (b) Take possession of the Project and complete construction
    (c) Exercise all remedies under the Mortgage and other Loan documents
    (d) Pursue Guarantor under the Completion Guaranty

ARTICLE 7 - INSURANCE REQUIREMENTS

7.1 Required Coverage. Borrower shall maintain:
    (a) Builder's risk insurance in the full replacement cost of improvements
    (b) Commercial general liability of not less than $5,000,000 per occurrence
    (c) Workers' compensation as required by law
    (d) Umbrella liability of not less than $25,000,000

ARTICLE 8 - GOVERNING LAW

8.1 This Agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of the Commonwealth of Massachusetts.
