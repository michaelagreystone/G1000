MEZZANINE LOAN AGREEMENT

This Mezzanine Loan Agreement ("Agreement") is made as of February 15, 2024, by and between:

LENDER: Blackstone Real Estate Debt Strategies LP, a Delaware limited partnership ("Mezzanine Lender" or "Lender")

BORROWER: Fallon Charlotte Holdings LLC, a Delaware limited liability company ("Mezzanine Borrower" or "Borrower")

RECITALS

WHEREAS, Borrower is the sole member of Fallon Charlotte Development LLC ("Property Owner"), which owns a 280-unit multifamily development project located at 1200 South Tryon Street, Charlotte, North Carolina 28203 (the "Property");

WHEREAS, Property Owner has obtained a senior mortgage loan from Wells Fargo Bank, N.A. in the amount of Forty-Five Million Dollars ($45,000,000.00) (the "Senior Loan");

WHEREAS, Borrower desires to obtain mezzanine financing to be secured by a pledge of Borrower's membership interest in Property Owner;

NOW, THEREFORE, the parties agree as follows:

ARTICLE 1 - LOAN AMOUNT AND TERMS

1.1 Principal Amount. Lender agrees to lend to Borrower the principal sum of Fifteen Million Dollars ($15,000,000.00) (the "Mezzanine Loan").

1.2 Interest Rate. The Mezzanine Loan shall bear interest at a fixed rate of eleven percent (11%) per annum.

1.3 Payment Terms. Interest only payments shall be due monthly during the term. Principal shall be due at maturity.

1.4 Loan Term. The initial term shall be three (3) years from the date hereof, coterminous with the Senior Loan.

1.5 Extension Options. Two (2) one-year extension options, subject to:
    (a) No Event of Default
    (b) Minimum DSCR of 1.20x
    (c) Payment of 0.25% extension fee

ARTICLE 2 - TOTAL CAPITALIZATION

2.1 Capital Stack.
    Senior Loan:      $45,000,000   (60% LTC)
    Mezzanine Loan:   $15,000,000   (20% LTC)
    Equity:           $15,000,000   (20% LTC)
    ─────────────────────────────────
    Total:            $75,000,000   (100%)

2.2 Combined LTV. The combined loan-to-value of the Senior Loan and Mezzanine Loan shall not exceed eighty percent (80%).

ARTICLE 3 - SECURITY

3.1 Pledge of Membership Interests. The Mezzanine Loan shall be secured by a first priority pledge of 100% of Borrower's membership interest in Property Owner.

3.2 Assignment of Distributions. Borrower assigns all distributions from Property Owner to Lender as additional collateral.

3.3 UCC Filing. Lender shall file UCC-1 financing statements to perfect its security interest.

ARTICLE 4 - SUBORDINATION AND INTERCREDITOR

4.1 Subordination. The Mezzanine Loan is subordinate to the Senior Loan in right of payment and security.

4.2 Intercreditor Agreement. Lender and Senior Lender have entered into an Intercreditor Agreement governing:
    (a) Notice requirements
    (b) Cure rights
    (c) Standstill periods
    (d) UCC foreclosure procedures

4.3 Cure Rights. Lender shall have the right (but not obligation) to cure any default under the Senior Loan.

4.4 Standstill. Upon Senior Lender default notice, Lender shall have a sixty (60) day standstill period before exercising remedies.

ARTICLE 5 - COVENANTS

5.1 DSCR Covenant. Borrower shall maintain a minimum Debt Service Coverage Ratio of 1.15x, tested quarterly on a trailing twelve-month basis.

5.2 Occupancy Covenant. The Property shall maintain minimum physical occupancy of 90%.

5.3 Distributions. Prior to Mezzanine Loan repayment:
    (a) Distributions for Senior Loan debt service are permitted
    (b) Distributions for approved operating expenses are permitted
    (c) All other distributions require Lender consent

5.4 Change of Control. No change in the direct or indirect ownership of Borrower or Property Owner without Lender consent.

5.5 Additional Debt. No additional debt at the Borrower or Property Owner level without Lender consent.

ARTICLE 6 - PREPAYMENT

6.1 Lockout Period. The Mezzanine Loan may not be prepaid during the first twelve (12) months.

6.2 Prepayment Premium. Thereafter, prepayment is permitted subject to the following premiums:
    Months 13-24: 2.0% of outstanding principal
    Months 25-36: 1.0% of outstanding principal
    Thereafter: No premium

6.3 Open Period. The final three (3) months of the term shall be open for prepayment without premium.

ARTICLE 7 - DEFAULT AND REMEDIES

7.1 Events of Default.
    (a) Failure to pay principal or interest when due
    (b) Breach of any covenant not cured within applicable grace period
    (c) Default under the Senior Loan not cured by Lender
    (d) Bankruptcy or insolvency of Borrower, Property Owner, or Guarantor
    (e) Material adverse change in the Property or Borrower's financial condition

7.2 Remedies. Upon Event of Default, Lender may:
    (a) Accelerate all amounts due
    (b) Exercise UCC remedies, including foreclosure on the pledged membership interests
    (c) Take control of Property Owner
    (d) Pursue the Guaranty
    (e) Exercise all other available legal remedies

7.3 UCC Foreclosure. Lender may conduct a public or private sale of the membership interests in accordance with UCC Article 9.

ARTICLE 8 - GUARANTY

8.1 Recourse Carve-Out Guaranty. The Fallon Company, LLC, shall provide a recourse guaranty covering:
    (a) Fraud, willful misconduct, or material misrepresentation
    (b) Misapplication of insurance proceeds or condemnation awards
    (c) Waste
    (d) Environmental liabilities
    (e) Voluntary bankruptcy filing

8.2 Completion/Carry Guaranty. Guarantor guarantees completion of any remaining construction and payment of debt service shortfalls during lease-up.

ARTICLE 9 - REPORTING

9.1 Quarterly Reports. Within thirty (30) days of each quarter end:
    (a) Property operating statements
    (b) Rent roll
    (c) DSCR calculations
    (d) Occupancy reports

9.2 Annual Reports.
    (a) Audited financial statements of Borrower and Guarantor
    (b) Annual budget for following year
    (c) Updated appraisal (if requested)

ARTICLE 10 - GOVERNING LAW

10.1 This Agreement shall be governed by the laws of the State of New York.
