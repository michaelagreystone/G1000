DEAL MEMO: Nashville Gulch Hotel & Residential Mixed-Use
Project: The Soundstage at The Gulch
Location: 11th Avenue South, The Gulch, Nashville TN
===============================================================

EXECUTIVE SUMMARY
-----------------
The Soundstage is a mixed-use development featuring a 225-key upscale 
select-service hotel (Marriott AC brand) over ground-floor retail, with an 
adjacent 165-unit multifamily tower. The project leverages Nashville's 
exceptional population growth and tourism economy.

PROJECT COMPONENTS
------------------
Hotel Component:
- Keys: 225
- Brand: AC Hotel by Marriott (franchise)
- Floors: 12 stories
- Meeting Space: 4,500 SF
- Restaurant/Bar: 5,200 SF (hotel-operated)
- Fitness/Pool: 3,800 SF

Multifamily Component:
- Units: 165
- Mix: 70% 1BR (800 SF avg), 30% 2BR (1,150 SF avg)
- Rentable SF: 149,175 SF
- Amenities: Rooftop deck, co-working, fitness

Retail Component:
- Ground Floor: 12,000 SF
- Tenants: F&B focus (3-4 spaces)
